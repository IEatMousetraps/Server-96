Who We Are

Server 96 is a Columbus Crew supporters group that started on the Discordecke — the official Discord server for Crew fans. What began as an online community grew into a real-life crew that shows up together at tailgates and in the Nordecke to cheer on the Black and Gold.

Our Origins

Founded by moderators of the Discordecke Discord server, Server 96 was born out of a desire to bring online Crew fans together in person — and to grow the community of supporters who bleed Black and Gold.

The Nordecke

Many of our members gather in the Nordecke supporter section at Lower.com Field, though members sit all over the stadium. We're united by the scarf, the song, and the love of Columbus Crew SC.

Tailgates

Before every home match, Server 96 members link up for tailgates. Whether you're a longtime Crew fan or just discovering MLS, you'll find a welcoming group ready to chant alongside you.

The Discordecke

Our roots are digital. Join the Discordecke to connect with Crew fans year-round — match discussions, transfer talk, memes, and more. It's where this whole thing started.
